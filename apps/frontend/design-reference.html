Section 08

Dark mode & Sage Smoke palette

CloudNow ships two dark modes: the default sober slate (slate-900 base) and the optional Sage Smoke variant (green-undertone blacks). The toggle in the header cycles light → dark → sage-smoke → light. The Sage Smoke palette is activated with [data-theme="dark"][data-palette="sage-smoke"].
